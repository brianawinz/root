Atlantic Records

Music has always been. It is something that mankind has created to make a positive and negative imppact. Music is vocal or instrumental sounds (or both) combined in such a way as to produce beauty of form, harmony, and expression of emotion.It expresses many emotions from happiness, sadness, anger, or othing at all. Music is a positive ad negative impact on society today. We want to bring a positive vibes thats why we started Briana's Music Company.

Atlantic Records was founded in 1947 by Ahmet Ertegun and Herb Abramson. They both were fans of music and wanted to impact the music industry. Their passion for artist around the country made Atlantic Records the home for legends in the music industry. With over 60 years of recorded music history, our passion for artistry in music continues today as a new generation of incredible artists including Bruno Mars, Coldplay, fun., Jason Mraz, Ed Sheeran, Wiz Khalifa, Janelle Monáe, Skrillex, Trey Songz, Hunter Hayes and more continue to further the Atlantic Records legacy.
